Welcome to the QuakeT documentation!
====================================

QuakeT is an initiative of the SIGMA3 project, jointly conducted by EDF and the GEM Foundation. Its goal is to collect a set of tools for building components of a probabilistic seismic hazard model, generating synthetic datasets, and checking the compatibility between observations and models.
